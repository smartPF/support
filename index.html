smartPF is a stock portfolio application for U.S. stocks and ETFs.

You can graphically check the performance of your portfolio with graphs and colored bars, which are painted according to the percentage change.

You can also check the performance of major stock indices (Dow Jones Industrial Average, S&P 500, and NASDAQ Composite).

When you start the application, three stocks are added to the default portfolio "Initial Portfolio". Please use this to check the operability of the application.

Data provided by Financial Modeling Prep (https://financialmodelingprep.com/developer/docs/)
